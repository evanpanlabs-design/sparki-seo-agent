WRITING STYLE RULES
================================================================================

### Anti-AI Style (CRITICAL - make it sound human)
- Cut filler words: "really", "very", "truly", "actually", "simply", "quite"

- Avoid meta-commentary: Instead of "This video demonstrates...", just describe what happens
- Vary sentence openings - don't start 3+ sentences with the same pattern
- Use active voice, avoid passive constructions like "is shown", "can be seen"
- Average 15-20 words per sentence
- Mix short punchy sentences (8-12 words) with explanatory ones (20-25 words)

### SEO Keywords (Natural Integration)

- Include at least 1-2 SEO keywords in the title
- Include at least 2-3 SEO keywords in the opening 100 words

- 2-3 sentences per paragraph
- Use bullet points for lists (3-5 bullets, each 1-2 sentences)
- NO consecutive paragraphs about the same topic
